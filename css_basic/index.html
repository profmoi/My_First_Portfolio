Big30 Farms

About us

Big30 Farm is an agricultural enterprise committed to producing high-quality and affordable food products, contributing to the well-being of individuals and communities. Our motto, "Everyday Living," encapsulates our goal to provide food that enhances daily life while supporting various aspects of human development.

Mission

At Big30 Farm, we strive to:

  • Ensure the availability of nutritious, high-quality, and affordable food.
  • Foster human development through initiatives in education, sports, and technology.
  • Create sustainable agricultural practices that benefit both people and the environment.

Vision

To be a leading agricultural business that not only feeds the nation but also drives development across education, sports, and technology, positively impacting lives on a daily basis.

Our Involvements

Big30 Farm is more than just a farm. Our involvement stretches across various areas that contribute to human development and well-being:

Agriculture

Our core focus is to produce quality food that is affordable to people across all demographics. With sustainable farming practices and innovation, we aim to reduce food insecurity and promote healthy living.

Sports

Big30 Farm actively promotes sports as a way to foster unity and physical health. We organize:

  • School Competitions Encouraging students to participate in various sporting activities, helping them develop both their teamwork and individual skills
  • Community Cup: A sports competition that brings together various communities, enhancing social interaction and fostering healthy competition.
  • Sports Competitions:Sports Competitions: Supporting both amateur and semi-professional athletes by creating platforms for them to showcase their talents.

Education

We believe that education is the foundation of a thriving society. Big30 Farm supports education through:

  • Scholarship Exams: Providing financial support to students who excel academically, ensuring they have the resources to continue their studies.
  • Mentorship Programs: Offering guidance to schoolchildren to help them achieve academic and personal growth.
  • Scholar-Athlete Scholarships:We reward students who excel both academically and in sports, nurturing the development of well-rounded individuals.

Technology

  • IT TrainingIT Training: Offering courses and training sessions that equip individuals with skills in information technology, fostering digital literacy
  • Community Cup: A sports competition that brings together various communities, enhancing social interaction and fostering healthy competition.
  • Proficiency Programs:Training people to become proficient in key tech skills that will improve their employability and economic potential.
